The Gordon Ramsay Burger

Holy Hell That's Delicious!

It's flipping RAW! Or so that is until this all beef patty is cooked to absolute perfection - in this Gordon Romsay designed perfect cheese burger

    Ingredients
  • Beef Pattty
  • Brioche Buns
  • Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 large tomato
    Steps
  1. Make the patty
  2. Cook it to perfection
  3. Melt the cheese onto it
  4. Put that on the bun
  5. Slice the tomato to desired thickness
  6. Put it all together
  7. Enjoy!
