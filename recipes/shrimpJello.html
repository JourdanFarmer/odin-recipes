Shrimp Jell-O Recipe

Your Dream Come True!

You know those days when you just can't seem to solve that craving you have for a delicious, gelatinous, shrimpy bowl mould?

    Ingredients
  • Jell-O of your desired flavor (Grape is my favorite!)
  • Shrimp (the bigger, the better!)
  • Your wifes favorite decorative china
    Steps
  1. Mix the Jello-O with a clenched fist
  2. After your dog finishes licking the frost off the shrimp, place them into the solution
  3. Pour the mixture into your china and let cool in the beer cave of your favorite rest-stop
  4. Enjoy!
